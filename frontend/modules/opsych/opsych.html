About OPSYCH - Social Engineering Intelligence

OPSYCH is a comprehensive human intelligence platform that maps psychological and behavioral vulnerabilities across digital footprints.

What OPSYCH Does

  • Aggregate and analyze social media presence across platforms
  • Calculate human exposure scores based on public information
  • Simulate social engineering attack chains (SE Kill Chain)
  • Identify psychological manipulation vectors

Who It's For

  • Executive protection teams
  • Law enforcement and investigators
  • Corporate security operations
  • Red teams and penetration testers
  • High-net-worth individuals assessing personal risk

Core Capabilities

Social Media Search: Deep OSINT across Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and more.

Exposure Analysis: Quantify how much exploitable information a target reveals publicly.

Attacker Simulation: See exactly how a social engineer would weaponize discovered information.

Current Status

Beta - We're actively developing and improving based on user feedback. This module focuses on human vulnerabilities, complementing GHOST's technical credential intelligence.
