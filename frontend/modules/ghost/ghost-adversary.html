About Adversary Intelligence

Adversary Intelligence is designed to give organizations clarity into the actual adversaries that will most likely try to attack them and how they would do it.

What This Module Does:

  • Threat Actor Matching: Identifies which Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) groups are most likely to target your organization based on industry, geography, technology stack, and other factors
  • Attack Pattern Analysis: Shows the specific tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) these groups use, mapped to MITRE ATT&CK framework
  • Intelligence Sources: Draws from 800+ known APT groups across MISP Galaxy, MITRE ATT&CK, and curated threat intelligence feeds
  • Prioritized Defense: Helps you focus defensive efforts on the threats you're most likely to face

Why This Matters:

Not all threats are equally likely to target your organization. By understanding which adversaries are most relevant to you, you can:

  • Prioritize security investments effectively
  • Train your team on relevant attack patterns
  • Configure defenses against specific TTPs
  • Make informed risk decisions
