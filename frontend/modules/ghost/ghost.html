About GHOST - Credential Intelligence Module

GHOST is a comprehensive credential intelligence platform designed for security researchers, pentesters, and organizations of all sizes.

Our Mission

Provide enterprise-grade threat intelligence tools that are accessible to solo researchers and freelancers, not just large corporations with massive budgets.

What GHOST Does

  • Search across multiple breach databases and dark web sources
  • Match threat actors targeting your organization
  • Deploy honeytokens to detect active reconnaissance

Who It's For

  • Security researchers and bug bounty hunters
  • Penetration testers and red teams
  • Security operations centers (SOCs)
  • Small to enterprise organizations

Pricing Philosophy

In the full release, GHOST will offer tiered pricing to ensure accessibility - from free tiers for researchers to enterprise plans for organizations. Intelligence shouldn't be locked behind unaffordable paywalls.

Current Status

Beta - We're actively developing and improving based on user feedback.
